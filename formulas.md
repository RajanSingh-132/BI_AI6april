Flexible Budget Variance (FBV) = (Actual Volume × Actual Mix × Actual CM) − (Actual Volume × Actual Mix × Standard CM)

Sales Mix Variance (SMV) = (Actual Volume × Actual Mix × Standard CM) − (Actual Volume × Standard Mix × Standard CM)

Sales Quantity Variance (SQV) = (Actual Volume × Standard Mix × Standard CM) − (Standard Volume × Standard Mix × Standard CM)

Total Sales Variance (TSV) = FBV + SMV + SQV

Revenue Variance (RV) = Revenue Actual − Revenue Target

Revenue Actual (RA) = Price × Quantity

## Revenue Target (RT) = Planned Revenue

Cost of Goods Sold (COGS) = Beginning Inventory + Purchases − Ending Inventory

Contribution Margin (CM) = Selling Price − Variable Cost

Gross Margin Percentage (GMP) = ((Revenue − COGS) / Revenue) × 100

Churn Rate Percentage = (Customers Lost / Customers At Start) × 100

Monthly CRP = (Customers lost in month / Customers at start of month) × 100

Net Promoter Score (NPS) = %Promoters − %Detractors

% Promoters = (Number of promoters/Total Respondents)*100

% Detractors = (Number of Detractors/total Respondents)*100

Finance Score = (Revenue Actual / Revenue Target) × 100 (If the score exceeds 100, cap it at 100)

Customer Score = (NPS / 60) × 100

Customer Positive Effort Score = (No. of Positive Result / Total Respondents) * 100

Customer Negative Effort Score = (No. of Negative Result / Total Respondents) * 100

Customer Effort Score = (Pct of CPES - Pct of CNES)

Operations Score = (Employee Satisfaction / 10) × 100

Business Health Index (BHI) = (Finance Score × 0.40) + (Customer Score × 0.40) + (Operations Score × 0.20)
